Technology Revolution (Advanced portability)
-Remote working
Author Name

One of the cornerstones of the remote work revolution is the
proliferation of communication and collaboration tools.
Platforms like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Slack have
become indispensable for virtual meetings, real-time
messaging, and collaborative project management.

Cloud computing has played a pivotal role in enabling
remote work by providing a scalable and secure
infrastructure. Cloud-based services allow employees
to access data, applications, and resources from any
location with an internet connection.
